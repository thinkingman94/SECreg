INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON THE

NIGERIAN COMMODITIES MARKET
Date: 4th - 5th March, 2020

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE REGISTER

THEME:

Commodities Trading  Ecosystem:
Key to Diversifying  the Nigerian Economy

SEC Nigeria is pleased to host the international conference on the Nigerian commodities market come 4th - 5th March 2020.

The Securities and Exchange Commission in collaboration with the Commodities Trading Ecosystem Implementation Committee is working with stakeholders to implement the report of the technical committee on Commodities Trading Ecosystem. One of the recommendations of the report is to organize a round table event that brings together key stakeholders to have discussions in developing the commodities market. The event is expected to galvanize efforts to identify and address challenges facing the market and lay foundation for vibrant and functioning market that ensures price discovery and transparency.

ABOUT CONFERENCE

As part of efforts to revamp the commodities market, the Nigerian Capital Market community developed a Roadmap to revive activities in the commodities trading ecosystem. The Roadmap, which identified a four-phased implementation approach, recommended amongst others the convening of an international stakeholders’ conference in March 2020.
 
The theme for the conference is “Commodities Trading Ecosystem: Key to Diversifying the Nigerian Economy”. The conference would create a platform for indigenous stakeholders’ in the commodities trading ecosystem to discuss pertinent issues hindering the growth of the sector.
 
The Commodities market, if properly harnessed to its full potentials, can serve as a major catalyst for economic growth
